What is Photography?

Photography is the art, application, and practice of creating images by recording light. It allows us to capture moments, tell stories, and document history. There are various types of photography, including portrait, landscape, wildlife, and street photography.

Mastering photography requires an understanding of composition, lighting, and camera settings. Whether you're an amateur or a professional, photography is a powerful medium for creative expression.
